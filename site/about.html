About View From Elsewhere

Hi ! I’m Antonin and View From Elsewhere is an online gallery that retraces some of my video game adventures. I play, screenshot, edit and finally publish here.

With this website, I want to offer a fresh view on the games I capture and compose with, a new perspective, a frozen one, like a photographer of the virtual worlds.

Needless to say that HUGE and MASSIVE credits go to the visual artists and technical teams behind every game. Without them, nothing to recompose, nothing to remember.

Keyboard Shortcuts

A few shortcuts are available while browsing the gallery:

EOpen / close the Explore navigation
EscClose the Explore navigation or the photo viewer
← →Previous / next photo in the viewer
DDownload the current photo
EnterGo to the album of the current photo
